David Wiley
Institutionalising OERs

David's fellowship focus is Lumen Learning, a service provider to educational institutions that helps them adopt, adapt and optimise their use of open educational resources (OER) in order to improve quality and reduce costs.

We support David's work because if OER is going to fulfil its promise, it needs to be widely adopted, institutionalised and continuously improved. This can only be done with dedicated support and proper measurement.

Aneen Vernon
Business Services Manager

Aneen Vernon joined the Shuttleworth Foundation in March 2013, after spending 7 years in Malawi fulfilling financial and operations management roles of international import/export companies.

Aneen is responsible for the management of the Foundation finances and fellowship funding. Aneen holds a B.Com degree from the University of Stellenbosch and has a wealth of business management experience in a variety of industries covering retail, mining, manufacturing, legal, security and shipping.

She is passionate about her family, enjoys travelling and reading, and always welcomes a new challenge.

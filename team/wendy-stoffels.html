Wendy Stoffels
Office Manager

Wendy Stoffels holds the back office of the Foundation together. She channels all first contact communications including any funding requests and applications for the Fellowship Program and manages the daily admin of the office.

Wendy has nearly forty years of experience in working with non-governmental organisations, including 15 years spent at the University of the Western Cape. She has worked with communities for many years and has a strong background in administration, supervising, finance, coordinating events and international conferences.

Wendy loves travelling and her grandchildren are her pride and joy.
